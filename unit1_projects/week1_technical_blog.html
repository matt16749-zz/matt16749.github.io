Fiverr

Fiverr is an open-market website that allows people to offer their services for the minimum of $5. What's unique about this site is the services offered. You can find things such as online marketing, to hiring a graphics designer, impersonations of celebrities, and even hiring someone to flirt with you on facebook.

When you first visit the Fiverr page, your eyes will quickly focus in on the amount of services offered just on the landing page. As you peruse your optioons, you will invariably become attracted to the links that sort these services into similar utilities

Fiverr is unique in that it may solve none or all of your problems! If you come in with a problem, chances are that you will find a solution on Fiverr. If not, then your problem may be bigger than you have imagined.

Kickstarter

Kickstarter is a popular crowdsourcing website where people can gather and fund various projects. From 3d printing, to creating a indie record, everything imaginable is being offered. The most famous of these projects is the Veronica Mars movie that was fully funded April 2013. Take a look at the various offerings for yourself!
