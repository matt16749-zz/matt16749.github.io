Fiverr

Fiverr is trying to create a visually appealing marketplace for cheap services online. In creating a marketplace, Fiverr needed to make sure not to overload the user with too many services and also figure out a simple way to organize the services. Fiverr also needed to educate the new user on it's uniqueness and usabiliity.

Kickstarter

Kickstarter is trying to create a crowdfunding marketplace that gives incentives for users to fund various projects. Kickstarter had to appeal to the funding users by introducing projects with corresponding incentives as well as the project users in how to offer incentives.
