Marionette.js

Marionette + Backbone

Backbone is unopinionated and trying to figure out what function belongs to which component and how to modularize your code(as you'll have alot of boilerplate for your various views, models, and collections) can be a pain. Enter Marionette.js, a composite application framework built for Backbone.js.

    Marionette will...
  • allow you to scale Backbone applications.
  • Eliminate boilerplate view code with specialized views.
  • Give more components than the standard that comes with Backbone.
